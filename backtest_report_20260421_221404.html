Methodology

Data: Resolved binary Yes/No markets from Polymarket Gamma API. Price history from CLOB API (daily fidelity). Only markets with confirmed resolution (outcomePrices = [1,0] or [0,1]) are included.

Entry simulation: For each strategy × entry timing (T-7d, T-3d, T-1d before close), the YES token price from CLOB history is used as the entry price. If no price history is available for that window, the market is skipped.

Sizing: Flat = $10 per trade regardless of confidence. Kelly = half-Kelly stake on a $1,000 starting bankroll, assuming 15% edge over entry price, capped at 15% of bankroll per trade.

Key metric — Edge: Actual win rate minus the average entry price (= market-implied win probability). Positive edge means the strategy genuinely outperforms the market's pricing and is the primary signal for real-money viability.

Limitations: Entry prices are reconstructed from daily CLOB snapshots — intraday slippage is not modelled. Polymarket charges 0.1–0.2% taker fee (not included). Markets with no price history in the entry window are excluded.
